Overview

 

This page provides data products associated with the thermal Sunyaevâ€“Zeldovich (tSZ) power spectrum measurement using observations from the SPTpol/SPT-3G/Herschel-SPIRE surveys over roughly 100 square degrees described in Raghunathan et al. 2026. The tSZ power spectra are presented over the multipole range 500 < ℓ <= 5000.

Maps

 

Below are links to FITS files containing maps of the tSZ effect reconstructed using observations from multiple frequency bands using SPT and Herschel-SPIRE surveys. Specifically, the observations include 150 GHz data from SPTpol (2012-2016 observing season); 95, 150, and 220 GHz from SPT-3G (2019-20 observing season); and 600 and 857 GHz data from Herschel-SPIRE survey.

The patch size is 100 sqaure degrees and the white noise levels correspond to 4.5, 3, and 16 uK-arcmin at 95, 150, and 220 GHz bands. The tSZ maps are reconstructed using the harmonic space linear combination technique which combines information from the all the above freqeuncy bands to either reduce (a) the overall variacne from noise and foreground variance called the minimum-variance (MV) technique and (b) the contamination from a given foreground signal which in our case is the cosmic infrarred background (CIB) minimised (CIB-min) map.

(Fig. 1 of Raghuanthan et al. 2026.) The above image shows the MV tSZ map reconstruction. The top right inset panel shows the stack of galaxy clusters and the bottom right inset panel shows a zoom-in of one of the locations with multiple galaxy clusters seen as red dots.

Compton-y maps and masks:

  • maps_and_masks/masks.npy. This contains both the boundary mask and the point source mask. Point source mask is used to mask all sources with flux at 150 GHz S150 ≥ 2 mJy.
  • maps_and_masks/ymv_full_proj0.fits: This is the MV map from the full dataset.
  • maps_and_masks/ymv_half1_proj0.fits: MV map from half1.
  • maps_and_masks/ymv_half2_proj0.fits: MV map from half2.
  • maps_and_masks/ycibfree_full_proj0.fits: CIB-min map from the full dataset.
  • maps_and_masks/ycibfree_half1_proj0.fits: CIB-min map from half1.
  • maps_and_masks/ycibfree_half2_proj0.fits: CIB-min map from half2.
 

tSZ bandpowers
Fig. 4 of Raghuanthan et al. 2026 showing the yy bandpowers from the CIB-min map show as green along with other measurements from ACT/Planck, and SPT as well as the curves from FLAMINGO simulations (dotted: LS8 and dashed: LS8+fgas-8σ). In the below files, we release the bandpowers along with the covariance and the bandpower window function.

Bandpowers window function:

The spectra are binned with uniform weighting in Cℓ:

To bin theory Cl, you can do: cl_theory_binned = np.dot( bpwf, cl_theory_unbinned)
  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_cl_bpwf.txt. Bandpower window function. This is required to compare theory to the binned spectra. More details are inside the file.

Bandpowers: Columns are ℓ, Dℓ[1012], δDℓ[1012]. Note that none of these have the noise bias.

  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_dl_yy_bandpowers_ycibfreexycibfree.txt: Auto-power spectrum CIB-min x CIB-min
  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_dl_yy_bandpowers_ymvxymv.txt: Auto-power spectrum MV x MV
  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_dl_yy_bandpowers_ycibfreexycibfree.txt: Cross-power spectrum MV x CIB-min

Bandpower covariance matrices: Sum of both statistical and systematic errros.

To get the bandpower errors δDℓ[1012], you can do: np.sqrt( np.diag( dl_yy_cov ) )
  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_dl_yy_cov_ycibfreexycibfree.txt: Covariance for CIB-min x CIB-min
  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_dl_yy_cov_ymvxymv.txt: Covariance for MV x MV
  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_dl_yy_cov_ycibfreexycibfree.txt: Covariance for MV x CIB-min
 

tSZ x CIB correlation

     

Fig. 7 and Fig. 8 of Raghuanthan et al. 2026 showing the reconstructed tSZ x CIB correlation. In the below files, we release the scale-dependent tSZ x CIB cross-correlation coefficients.

tSZ x CIB cross-correlation coefficient: Columns are ℓmin, ℓmax, ℓeff, ρℓtSZxCIB, δρℓtSZxCIB

  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_rho_tsz_cib_diff_cibmin_mv.txt: Reconstructed using Diff(CIB-min, MV)
  • bandpowers_and_cov/spt_100d_rho_tsz_cib_diff_cibmin_mvxcibmin.txt: Reconstructed using Diff(CIB-min, MVxCIB-min)
